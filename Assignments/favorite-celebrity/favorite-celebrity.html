One Direction

The beginning of One Direction

One Direction was originally formed as a five-member band in X-Factor 2010. The five members, Harry Styles, Liam Payne, Louis Tomlinson, Niall Horan, and Zayn Malik, first auditioned as solo candidates, before the judges decided to put them together in a band.

The band got together for two weeks to get to know each other and rehearse. Harry Styles came up with the band's name.

Post X-Factor

One Direction debuted with the first album Up All Night during 2011-2012. Their hit single What Makes Your Beautiful was an international success and dominated the UK singles chart.



One of the biggest boybands of all time

Shortly, One Direction became one of the biggest boybands of all time. They released 5 albums in 6 years, selling million of copies in the process.

One Direction's discography throughout the years:

In December 2013, One Direction released a movie named This Is Us. Nearly 270,000 copies of the film were sold in the UK within three days of its release, beating the record previously set by Michael Jackson's This Is It in 2010 by 10,000 copies.

Zayn Malik leaves the band

On 25 March 2015, amid the Asia leg of their tour, the band announced Malik's departure with an official statement. I'd like to apologise to the fans if I've let anyone down, but I have to do what feels right in my heart, Malik said in the statement. I am leaving because I want to be a normal 22-year-old who is able to relax and have some private time out of the spotlight. I know I have four friends for life in Louis, Liam, Harry and Niall. I know they will continue to be the best band in the world.

After Malik's departure, One Direction resumed their journey as a four-member band for another year.

One Direction Goes on indefinite Hiatus

In August 2015, the group said that it would go on hiatus in 2016 to work on individual projects but that they will remain together and plan to work together in the future.
